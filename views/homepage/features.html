Thimble is a full-featured code editor that runs right in the browser. It's aimed at creating web sites that include HTML, CSS & Javascript. We've geared it's features towards beginners, allowing you (or your classroom) to get up and running as quickly as possible.

It's a code editor, web server, web browser & dev tools all in one—and it's free!

Instantly see your changes

See your changes instantly as you edit your project files. There's no need to refresh the preview. Makes learning about CSS really easy!

Remix any project

All published Thimble proejecs can be remixed. Send your project to your friends or students and see what they can come up with? See something you like, then make your own version!

Add and manage files

Thimble is works like a real web project, you can create, raname, delete and even upload your own files including folders. Makes it easy to work with your own images and photos. Or to open your old projects.

  • Download a .zip of your entire project
  • Drag & drop files like photos from your computer into Thimble

Light & Dark Themes

Thimble comes with a dark and light theme, you can toggle it to match your preference.

Embedded Tutorials

Projects can have tutorials that help you (or your students) learn as they make changes to the projects the Remix.
