Why Contribute?

  • Your work impacts users all over the world.
  • Gain valuable experience and mentorship.
  • Support mozilla's mission of an open internet for all.
  • If feels good!

Thimble is an open source project created by the Mozilla Foundation in parnership with CDOT at Seneca College to help people learn HTML, CSS & Javascript by creating their own web-based projects.
