Why Contribute?

  • Your work impacts users all over the world.
  • Gain valuable experience and mentorship.
  • Support mozilla's mission of an open internet for all.
  • If feels good!

Thimble is an Open Source project created by the Mozilla Foundation in parnership with CDOT at Seneca College. Since the beginning, contributors have played an important role in making it a success. If you'd like to get involved, check out the links below.

If you find an issue you want to work on, just leave a comment on it and we'll be in touch.
